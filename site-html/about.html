At Hughes Automotive Solutions, I am more than just an autobody repair shop – I am your trusted neighbor and dedicated automotive specialist. Founded and operated by myself, I am proud to serve our local community from my garage right here in Enfield.

Our Story

About Zack

With a passion for cars and a commitment to excellence, I bring over 10 years of experience to every project. As a skilled autobody technician, I have honed my craft through hands-on experience and ongoing training, ensuring that every repair meets the highest standards of quality and safety.

Mission

At Hughes Automotive Solutions, our mission is simple: to provide exceptional autobody repair services with a personal touch. We understand that your vehicle is more than just a mode of transportation – it’s a vital part of your daily life. That’s why we approach every repair with the care and attention to detail that your car deserves.

What Sets Us Apart

As a local business, we take pride in our close-knit community and strive to build lasting relationships with our customers. When you choose Hughes Automotive Solutions, you’re not just another number – you’re a valued member of our extended family. We believe in transparent communication, honest pricing, and going the extra mile to ensure your complete satisfaction.

Our Services

From minor dents and scratches to major collision repairs, Hughes Automotive Solutions is equipped to handle all of your autobody needs. Using state-of-the-art tools and techniques, we’ll restore your vehicle to its pre-accident condition, leaving you with a finish that’s as good as new.
